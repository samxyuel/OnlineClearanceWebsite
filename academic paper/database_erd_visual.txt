KEY FEATURES:
- Sector-based clearance periods (College, SHS, Faculty)
- Automatic form creation when clearance period starts
- Signatory assignments managed through clearance signatories
- Program Head department-specific assignments
- Stored procedures for clearance period management
- Comprehensive audit logging and user activity tracking
